Own a Lot?

Whether you own or manage parking lots, FanPark is the ideal way to monetize your lot with FanPark registered drivers.

  • Make your lot accessible to your customers weeks before the event
  • Increase your revenue while building a loyal customer base
  • Reduce waiting time in lot transactions
  • Allow customers to pay seamlessly and securely through the Stripe payment gateway

How It Works:

Register and Verify

Register your parking lot on the FanPark app and connect your account to Stripe to receive payments.
Set your price, number of available spaces, and indicate any amenities (near bathrooms, etc.)

Open for Business

Once a FanPark driver reserves a parking space, you will receive an email confirmation.
On event day, drivers will show their confirmation number and you will direct them to their space.

Attract Drivers

We can provide you with signage you can display to encourage FanPark participation.
